How Atomic Swap Works

5 steps to a trustless cross-chain trade

1

Seller Creates Secret

Generates a random secret and publishes its SHA-256 hash. Shares DOGE receiving address.

2

Seller Locks BTCT

BTCT is locked in an on-chain HTLC contract with a 24-hour timeout. Only the secret can unlock it.

3

Buyer Locks DOGE

DOGE is locked in a P2SH HTLC script (12-hour timeout) using the same hash. Fully refundable on timeout.

4

Seller Claims DOGE

Seller reveals the secret to claim DOGE from the P2SH. The secret is now public on the blockchain.

5

Buyer Claims BTCT

Buyer uses the revealed secret to unlock the BTCT HTLC. Trade complete ‚Äî both got their coins.
